about me

A native Oregonian, Mären Burck finds inspiration for her designs in the marriage between the natural and mythical world around her. With primary stages focused on meditative repetition, her pieces unfold as fine designs that intrigue the imagination and intertwine American and Scandinavian folklore with childhood imagery.
